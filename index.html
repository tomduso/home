About

Since September 2024, I have served as Chairman of the German Monopolies Commission, of which I have been a member since July 2022. I am Head of the Firms and Markets Department at DIW Berlin and a professor of empirical industrial economics at TU Berlin.

I am a faculty member of the Berlin School of Economics and spokesperson of the Berlin Centre for Consumer Policies (BCCP). At the European level, I serve as a member of the Economic Advisory Group on Competition Policy (EAGCP) of the European Commission, and as a Research Fellow for CEPR and CESifo.

My research is in applied econometrics across industrial organization, competition policy, regulation, and management. My work has appeared in The Review of Economics and Statistics, Journal of the European Economic Association, The Economic Journal, European Economic Review, and Journal of Management Studies, among others.

I have advised numerous public bodies on competition policy — including several DGs of the European Commission, the UK Competition and Markets Authority, the Dutch Authority for Consumers and Markets, the OECD, and the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development.
